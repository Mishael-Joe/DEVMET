BUILD YOUR CAREER EXPERIENCE

DEVMET is a collaborative platform that enables developers to enhance their skillset by working in a team. On this platform, you can collaborate with Frontend developers, Backend developers, and UI/UX designers to create projects using Git and GitHub. DEVMET is an ideal platform for individuals seeking to acquire new skills, expand their network, or gain practical experience.

WHY DEVMET

One of the main challenges faced by aspiring tech professionals is INEXPERIENCE, which often proves to be a significant barrier to entry. Many companies are reluctant to hire individuals without prior experience due to the potential risk of mistakes that could prove costly. To address this issue, DEVMET offers a simulated work environment that enables novice developers to gain practical skills and knowledge while minimizing the risk of errors. By providing a platform for new and inexperienced developers to train and develop their abilities, DEVMET aims to support the growth of a robust and capable workforce in the tech industry.

LEARN

We offer a simulated work environment that allows you to make mistakes without risking your job, and instead receive corrective feedback.

BUILD

You'll collaborate with developers from various domains as a team, working on fascinating and thrilling ventures. Above all, you'll gain insights into the development of real-world projects and establish connections that will prove valuable in your professional journey.

GROWTH

We place great importance on your personal development in our simulated work environment. You will be responsible for producing top-notch code and conducting thorough testing. Additionally, you will gain practical experience using tools commonly utilized in actual projects.
